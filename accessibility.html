Accessibility Statement

At Campaign Store, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive and user-friendly experience for all visitors.

Our Commitment

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website by:

  • Ensuring our website is compatible with screen readers and other assistive technologies.
  • Providing alternative text for images and other non-text content.
  • Using clear and simple language to make our content easy to understand.
  • Designing our website to be navigable using a keyboard.
  • Ensuring sufficient color contrast for text and background elements.

Accessibility Features

Here are some of the accessibility features available on our website:

  • Keyboard Navigation: You can navigate our website using only your keyboard.
  • Alt Text: All images have descriptive alt text for screen readers.
  • Resizable Text: You can resize the text on our website using your browser's zoom feature.
  • High Contrast Mode: Our website supports high contrast mode for better readability.
